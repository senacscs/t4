Students Explore Nature at Parque Witeck

A day of learning, fresh air, and discovery guided by SENAC teachers

On June 11, 2025, students from SENAC visited Parque Witeck, located in Novos Cabrais, on a school field trip. The group left Santa Cruz do Sul at 8:00 a.m. and arrived at the park around 9:00 a.m. Even though the weather was cold, the morning was sunny, with a bit of fog and clean air, perfect for exploring nature.

Guided by teachers Carlos, Poliana, and Kauane, students walked through different areas full of trees, plants, and animals. They took part in a photo activity to capture the natural beauty and also looked closely at tree leaves to study their shapes and textures. The trip was not only educational but also a great chance to enjoy nature and spend time with classmates.
