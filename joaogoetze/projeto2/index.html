How to Reduce Piracy in Games

Introduction / Justification (Part 1)

Goal 10.2: By 2030, empower and promote the economic, social, and political inclusion of all.

Introduction / Justification (Part 2)

Game Exclusivity:
Game exclusivity as a key feature of luxury products. High prices justify exclusivity but limit access for consumers with lower purchasing power. (Hennings, Wiedmann, and Klarmann (2012))

Introduction / Justification (Part 3)

Consumer Perspective on:
Materialistic consumers have unethical consumption attitudes. (Kozar and Marcketti (2011)) Happiness related to the consumption of goods, even if they are not original (VEER; SHANKAR, 2011).

Research Problem

How does the localization of digital game prices to local purchasing power affect the incidence of piracy in a specific community?




Main Objective

Develop an interactive choice site that educates users about digital game piracy.

Hypothesis

The game will educate the people who play it.

Expected Results

The app will encourage interested parents to have their children play, and the children will learn more about the topic. A limitation of the game would be the marketing of my game.
