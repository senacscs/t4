Second-year students from SENAC SCS School visit Witeck Park

By SENAC NEWS Editorial | June 11 | Education

Last Friday, second-year technical course students from SENAC Santa Cruz do Sul had the opportunity to visit the renowned Witeck Park in an extracurricular activity that combined learning and fun.

The field trip, organized by Biology and Sociology teachers, aimed to provide students with practical experience of contact with nature and ecological awareness.

"It was amazing to see the students' excitement throughout the tour. They were able to apply many of the concepts we've seen in class in practice," said teacher Poliana, one of the activity organizers.

During the visit, students participated in various activities, including:

  • Guided ecological trail
  • Native species identification workshop
  • Environmental awareness activities
  • Sustainable picnic

The school administration has already announced that they are planning new outdoor activities for the coming months, continuing the experiential learning project that has been a success among students.
